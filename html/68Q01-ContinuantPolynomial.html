Continuant polynomial


It is defined as follow

Kn⁢(x1,x2,…,xn):={1,if⁢n=0;x1,if⁢n=1;x1⁢Kn-1⁢(x2,…,xn)+Kn-2⁢(x3,…,xn),if⁢n>1.

It is easy to show, that

Kn⁢(x1,x2,…,xn)=det⁡(xn+10…00-1xn-1+1…000-1xn-2⋱00⋮⋮⋱⋱⋮⋮000…x2+1000…-1x1).

References: Knuth D.E. ”The Art of Computer Programming, Volume 2: Seminumerical Algorithms.
