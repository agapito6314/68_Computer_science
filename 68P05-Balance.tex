The \PMlinkescapetext{(weight)} \emph{balance} $\rho(T)$ of a tree $T$ is the number of leaves of the 
left subtree of a tree, denoted 
$|T_l|$, divided by the total number of leaves of the tree. 
That is, $\rho (T) = \frac{|T_l|}{|T|}$.
